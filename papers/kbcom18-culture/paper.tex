\begin{abstract}
It is very common that people of different cultures hold different opinions on the same named entities. 
Knowledge about such cross-cultural differences of named entities in existing knowledge bases can benefit a lot of downstream applications, especially for Computational Social Science.
However, research on mining such knowledge is almost missing from the literature.
In this paper, we propose this novel research topic with a preliminary study on collecting datasets and proposing several approaches. 
\end{abstract}
